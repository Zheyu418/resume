Why we need sociology in our life

Sociology is everywhere in life.

Through sociology we can understand the evolution of intricate social structures and cultures. By studying sociology, we can gain insight into the reasons behind social problems, inequalities and cultural differences.

It enables individuals to gain a deeper understanding of the forces that influence our behaviors, relationships, and communities in order to navigate this complex world and build a more compassionate society.

Why I am making this website

By creating this site, I hope I can document some meaningful sociological discussions, look at current events from a sociological perspective, and think about how these events will affect our society and individuals.

Also, I am trying to foster a platform for reflection and dialogue on the intersections of society and contemporary issues.

I hope that sociology can become not only a specialized discipline, but also a gadget to help people make decisions in their lives. In the process of exposure to sociology, people will not only learn the theories of different schools of thought, but also gain a lot of practical skills.
